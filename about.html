Our Family & Workbench

[ Workshop & Artisan Action Photo Placeholder ]

Dandy Saddlery was built out of an absolute refusal to let standard, mass-produced plastic and low-grade leather dominate the field. Every tool wrapper, strap, and saddle tree out of our shop is given focused care.

We work closely with historic tanneries inside the country to select thick, durable vegetable harness stock that thrives under severe outdoor work environments. When you buy our work, you are purchasing an heirloom built to outlast generations.
